PREMIUM
prueba(
prweh
-
bah
)
A feminine noun is almost always used with feminine articles and adjectives (e.g. la mujer bonita, la luna llena).
feminine noun
1. (assessment)
a. test
Hay una prueba de matemticas el mircoles.There's a math test on Wednesday.
b. examination
No pas la prueba de acceso y tiene que tomar cursos de regularizacin.He didn't pass the entrance examination and has to take remedial courses.
c. quiz (education)
Regionalism used in the United States
(United States)
Nos han dado una prueba para hacer en casa.We've been given a quiz to do at home.
2. (demonstration)
a. proof
La carta fue la prueba de que su historia era cierta.The letter was proof that her story was true.
b. token
Te doy este anillo como prueba de mi amor.I give you this ring as a token of my love.
c. sign
Su risa fue la prueba de que ya no estaba enojada.Her laugh was a sign that she was no longer mad.
3. (legal)
a. piece of evidence
El fiscal no pudo presentar ninguna prueba para condenarlo.The prosecution couldn't present a single piece of evidence to convict him.
4. (medicine)
a. test
Tuvo que dar una muestra de orina para la prueba de drogas.He had to give a urine sample for the drug test.
5. (film and theater)
a. audition
El actor fue a una prueba para el papel principal.The actor went to an audition for the leading role.
b. screen test (for the big screen)
Jennifer estuvo impresionante en su primera prueba de cmara.Jennifer was stunning in her first screen test.
6. (sports)
a. event
Fuimos a ver las pruebas de descenso de los Juegos Olmpicos de invierno.We went to see the downhill events at the Winter Olympics.
b. heat (qualifying)
Solo quedaron dos participantes despus de las pruebas de clasificacin.There were only two participants left after the qualifying heats.
c. race (running competition)
Se est preparando para la prueba de los cien metros.He's training for the hundred meter race.
7. (sewing)
a. fitting
Solo le queda una prueba del vestido de novia.She only has one more fitting for her wedding dress.
8. (photography and printing)
a. proof
El fotgrafo mir las pruebas para escoger las fotos que quera poner en su sitio web.The photographer looked at the proofs to choose the photos he wanted to include on his website.
9. (test)
a. probation
Juan todava no tiene seguridad de empleo porque todava est en su perodo de prueba.Juan doesnt have job security yet, because hes still in his probation period.
b. trial
Las pruebas clnicas del nuevo medicamento han tenido xito.The clinical trials of the new medication have been successful.
c.
This refers to an idiomatic word or phrase for which there is no word-for-word translation.
no direct translation
Bernardo es un piloto de prueba para un fabricante de aviones.Bernardo is a test pilot for an airplane manufacturer.
No me gusta el pescado. - Pero nunca has probado como lo preparo yo. Haz la prueba.I don't like fish. - But you've never tried it the way I make it. Try it.
10. (sample)
a. taste
Me das una prueba de la salsa?Can I have a taste of the sauce?
11. (unpleasant experience)
a. ordeal
Le quedaron las huellas de la prueba dura del asalto.She was scarred by her ordeal of being attacked.
12. (mathematics)
a. check
No te olvides de hacer la prueba del resultado.Dont forget to do a check to confirm the answer.
b. proof
El estudiante se haba acordado de escribir Q.E.D. al final pero haba omitido uno de los pasos principales de la prueba.The student had remembered to write Q.E.D at the end but had left out one of the key steps of the proof.
pruebas
A plural noun indicates that there is more than one person, place, thing, or idea.
plural noun
13. (legal)
a. evidence
El juez debe pesar todas las pruebas presentadas antes de dictar sentencia.The judge must weigh all of the evidence presented before sentencing.
